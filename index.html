About me

I'm Adelina, I am currently an Artificial Intelligence bachelor's student at the University of Groningen. At the moment I am developing machine learning systems with applications in particle physics research. I like to expand on my skills in a project-based approach.

On top of my regular degree programe I am also part of the University honours collage, a programme focused on developing interdisciplinary teamwork between students and a drive to pursue their interests trough projects within their field of interest.

Outside academics I organize and lead mountain scout camps for youth. I am also an amateur photographer, I am particularly drawn to wildlife and astrophotography.

I like to keep an open and positive attitude about everything in life.

Research Interests

My research interests are in the area of machine learning applications, especially in the development of avionics, particle physics research and AI-enhanced medical systems.

Some specific topics I am interested in include:

  • Adaptive control
  • Computer vision
  • Reinforcement learning
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Explainable AI and Uncertainty Quantification

I am passionate about developing AI applications which come as a support to humans and science advancements.
