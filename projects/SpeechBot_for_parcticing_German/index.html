Speech Bot for practicing speaking German

April 2026 - Ongoing

This project is focused on developing a speech bot for practicing speaking German. The goal is to create a system that allows users to carry out conversations with the Bot and recive feedback and if needed correction of mistekes in the german phrases they pronounce. In case they wish to say something but don't know the vocabulary the bot will help them with the german translation of the english phrase they wish to say.

This work is currenly in progress. An update will follow after its completion, which is expected to be around the end of 2026.

This is a ilustarion that reminds me that I should practice german more often.
